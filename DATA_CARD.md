## ⚠️ Provenance statement

This dataset is **not real Vietnamese market data**. No licensed VNINDEX/
VN30 feed is connected to this environment (gap R1). It is a fully-specified,
seeded stochastic simulation calibrated to *publicly-documented* VN stylized
facts. It exists so decision quality can be measured against **known ground
truth** with statistical power. It must not be presented as, or confused
with, real history. Real-data cards must be authored when a feed is
connected.

## Known biases & limitations

- **Model risk:** the DGP is linear-factor + Gaussian-plus-jumps; real
  markets have richer non-linearities, time-varying premia, microstructure,
  transaction costs and liquidity constraints not modelled here.
- **No costs/slippage:** returns are gross; real net returns would be lower.
- **Stationary premia:** true factor premia are constant; real premia decay
  and rotate.
- **No survivorship/corporate-actions realism** beyond point-in-time VN30.

## Splits & reproducibility

Deterministic in seed. Monte-Carlo = 20 independent seeds. Walk-forward =
4 sequential time folds. All commands in `EXPERIMENT_REGISTRY.md`.
