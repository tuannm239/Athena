# Athena — Quick Start

Athena is a **Financial Decision Intelligence Platform**. It helps you make
better investment decisions through explainable, probabilistic, risk-aware
reasoning. **Athena assists; you approve.** It never executes trades and never
connects to a broker — it produces Decision Objects for a human to review.

## 1. Sign in

Open the app URL and sign in with the email + password your administrator
created for you. Sessions use short-lived tokens and refresh automatically.

## 2. Find your way around

The left sidebar groups everything:

- **Decisioning** — Dashboard, Decision Center, Companies, Portfolio
- **Intelligence** — Research, Knowledge Graph, Feature Store, Probability
- **Analysis** — Backtest, Scenario Simulator, Market, Reports
- **System** — Administration (admins), Settings, Profile

Press **⌘K** (macOS) or **Ctrl K** (Windows/Linux) anywhere to open the
**command palette** — search decisions, companies, reports and pages, or jump
straight to any screen. Press **?** to see all keyboard shortcuts.

## 3. Review a decision

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Pending Reviews**, or open the **Decision Center**.
2. Open a decision to see its hypothesis, probability, confidence, expected
   utility, **risk assessment**, and the **evidence** for and against it.
3. Read the evidence and the explanation, then **Approve** or **Reject** with a
   note. Every decision requires this human step — nothing is acted on until
   you approve it.

## 4. Stay informed

The **bell** in the top bar shows in-app notifications: decisions awaiting your
review, pipeline/provider issues, and system alerts. There is no email or SMS —
everything lives in the app.

## 5. Export & report

- On the Decision Center, Portfolio, or a decision's evidence, use **Export**
  to download **CSV, Excel, PDF, or JSON**.
- The **Reports** page generates Decision, Portfolio, Risk, and Daily/Weekly/
  Monthly reports as PDF or Excel — all produced in your browser.

## 6. Make it yours

In **Settings** you can switch theme (dark/light), density
(comfortable/compact), reduce motion, choose your landing page, and manage
**favorites**, **pinned companies**, and **recent items**.
