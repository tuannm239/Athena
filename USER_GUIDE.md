# Athena — User Guide (Web v1.0)

Athena is a **Financial Decision Intelligence** web application for the
Vietnamese market. You can run your whole daily investment routine inside it —
Athena assists, and **you** approve every decision. It executes no trades.

## Getting started

1. **Register or sign in** at the login screen (Register tab creates an
   account; passwords are at least 8 characters).
2. You land on the **Dashboard** — Vietnam indices, your portfolio, pending
   reviews, watchlist, market heatmap, recent evidence and system status.
3. Press **⌘K / Ctrl K** anywhere to search and jump to any page, decision or
   company. Press **?** for keyboard shortcuts.

## The daily loop

1. **Market** (`/market`) — VNINDEX/VN30/HNX, market breadth, foreign &
   proprietary flows, sector heatmap, and top gainers/losers/volume. Click a
   ticker to open its workspace.
2. **Companies** (`/companies`) — search a ticker or pick a popular/pinned one.
3. **Company Workspace** — one page, twelve tabs: Overview, Financial
   Statements, Ratios, Growth, Valuation, Research, Evidence, Decision, Risk,
   History, Notes, Peers. **Pin** (📌) or **favorite** (★) to add to your
   watchlist.
4. **Research** (`/research`) — upload documents (PDF/Excel/reports), keep
   notes, mark them human-reviewed (each note keeps an audit trail), and browse
   the evidence corpus by company/industry/sector/macro/regulations.
5. **Decision** — a decision carries an investment thesis, bull/bear cases,
   catalysts, risks, supporting & counter evidence, probability, confidence,
   expected utility, a timeline, and a Decision Journal. **Approve or reject**
   with a note — this human step is mandatory.
6. **Watchlist** (`/watchlist`) — your followed companies with upcoming
   quarterly/annual/AGM/dividend reminders.
7. **Portfolio** (`/portfolio`) — holdings, allocation, performance, sector
   exposure and cash.
8. **Reports** (`/reports`) — one-click Company/Decision/Portfolio/Research/
   Market and Daily/Weekly/Monthly reports, exportable to **PDF, Excel, CSV,
   JSON**.

## Tools

- **Probability Studio** — update a prior with evidence (Bayesian), live.
- **Backtest** — buy-and-hold equity curve + metrics.
- **Scenario Simulator** — stress-test your portfolio against a market shock.
- **Knowledge Graph** — your decisions and the evidence linking them.
- **Feature Store** — the catalogue of metrics Athena computes.

## Notifications & feedback

- The **bell** shows in-app notifications (review reminders, pipeline/provider/
  system alerts); the full list is at `/notifications`.
- **Feedback** (`/feedback`) — tell us what's working and what isn't.
- **Help** (`/help`), **About** (`/about`), **Release Notes** (`/release-notes`).

## Make it yours

**Settings** (`/settings`): theme (dark/light), density, language, high
contrast, reduce motion, landing page, favorites, pinned companies, recent
items. Sign out from the top-right.

## Good to know

Where a live Vietnamese feed is not yet connected, Athena shows clearly-labelled
**sample** data and switches to live automatically once the feed is on. Notes,
watchlist and preferences are saved in your browser; decisions and evidence are
stored on the server.

*Athena assists; you decide.*
